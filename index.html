Who we are:

We're a lightweight nomadic consultancy manned by Bucky Wolfe, with 10 years of experience in the development, design and maintenance of VoIP services in the Internet and Telecom sector. We take great pride in helping ISPs solve the challenges in providing VoIP services for business and residential customers. Building lightweight, secure, maintainable systems on top of proven open source software our philosophy.

Services we provide:

Initial design and deployment of VoIP Services:
Registrar, Media Services, Provisioning Servers, VQM, CDR Collection
Account provisioning for new client setups:
Manual provisioning for small scale deployments
Customer consultation for ensuring success with large deployments
VoIP Troubleshooting:
Debugging signaling and media quality issues from Traces / Packet Captures
Development:
Backend development in Ruby, Python, Go, or PHP
Integration projects with 2600hz, Asterisk AGI/AMI, Twilio, Cloud Provider APIs
General Sysadmin:
Deployment and Maintenance Linux or OpenBSD systems
Creation of custom configuration management assets (Chef or Ansible)
Ownership and Maintenance of Public Cloud infrastructure
